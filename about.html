Mahatma Gandhi Memorial High School

MISSION AND VISION

MGM HIGH SCHOOL

To achieve academic excellence and to nurture learning for life through integration of enduring values so that students become worthy and caring citizens of Fiji.

Nurturing the whole person through the head the heart and the hands.

MINISTRY OF EDUCATION

To cultivate holistic, relevant and inclusive learning experiences benchmarked to global practices that empower indipendent, responsible and critical learners enabling them to effectively adapt and address the nation’s changing socio-economic needs and enrich the nation’s future.

Empowering Fiji's Future: Nurturing innovative, lifelong learners and enriching lives through quality education delivery for all.
