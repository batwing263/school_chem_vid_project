WEBSITE’S PURPOSE

This website showcases three science projects carried out in school for the science video competition. It provides a comprehensive account of each project's journey, including key stages, challenges and solutions. The website highlights the innovations and results, offering insights into the methods used and the impact of the projects. Sharing detailed logs promotes STEM, learning, collaboration, and transparency and allows users to track progress. The website celebrates the students' achievements and is a valuable resource for future educational and scientific endeavours. Ultimately, by navigating through the website, you get to experience the various elements, which contain the essence of Mahatma Gandhi Memorial High School.
