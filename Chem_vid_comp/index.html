SCIENCE DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

The Science Department is pleased to report that, following the completion of three creative projects with a theme on renewability, our students now have a stronger grasp of sustainable methods. These initiatives emphasised the value of environmental conservation in our day-to-day lives while also showcasing the promise of renewable resources. Our students showed creativity and critical thinking via practical experimentation and research. We are thrilled to keep spearheading programs that encourage the people at our school to value conservation and renewability.
